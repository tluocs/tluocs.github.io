Dear Prospective Student,

Thank you for your interest in pursuing a PhD in CS/EE/CPE with me. Due to the high volume of applications I receive, I kindly request you to review the following criteria before contacting me:

  • Master's degree: Having a Master's degree is required with the only exception specified below. Additionally, you must have at least one first-authored publication in a decent venue (e.g., a recognized international conference or journal; local conferences/journals do not qualify).
    • Exception for Bachelor-degree holders: You must gradudate from a well-known university in your country with GPA>3.20, AND have at least one first-authored publication in a CORE rank A or A* venue.
  • English Proficiency (if English is not your native langugage):
    • Minimum subcategory score: TOEFL: 22 | IELTS: 6.5 | Duolingo: 110.
    • GRE (not mandatory but preferred) - Minimum score: V 150, Q 160, AW 3.
If you don't meet the above criteria, please do not contact me. Otherwise it's a waste of your precious time.
