Dear Prospective Student,

Thank you for your interest in pursuing a PhD in CS/CPE/EE with me (I am faculty eligible to advise students in these 3 programs at UK). Due to the high volume of applications I receive, I kindly request you to review the following criteria before reaching out:

  • Master's degree: Having a Master's degree is required with the only exception specified below. Additionally, you must have at least one first-authored publication in a decent venue (a well-recognized international conference or journal).
    • Exception for Bachelor-degree holders: You must gradudate from a well-known university with GPA>3.20, AND have at least one first-authored publication in a CORE rank A or A* venue.
  • English Proficiency:
    • Minimum score: TOEFL: 96 (min. sub. 22) | IELTS: 7 (min. sub. 6.5) | Duolingo: 120 (min. sub. 110).
    • GRE (not mandatory but preferred): minimum V 150, Q 160, AW 3.
If you don't meet the above criteria, please do not contact me - as that will be a waste of your precious time.

But if you do, please inlcude the following in your email:

  1. Fill out this table and paste (not attach) it into the email body (you don't have to write a lengthy letter).

  3. Your representative publications.
