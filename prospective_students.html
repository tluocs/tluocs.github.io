Selection Criteria:

  • I strongly prefer Master's students although I allow exceptions. Generally, you should:
    • Hold a Master's degree AND have at least one first-authored decent publication (publications in unknown venues do not count).
    • Exception: Hold a Bachelor's degree, have at least one first-authored top-tier publication, AND gradudated from a decent-ranking university in your country with GPA>3.20.
  • English Proficiency (for international students):
    • Minimum subcategory score: TOEFL: 22 | IELTS: 6.5 | Duolingo: 110.
    • GRE (not mandatory but preferred) - Minimum score: V 150, Q 160, AW 3.
